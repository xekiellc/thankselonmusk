SpaceX

Founded 2002 · Hawthorne, California

Multi-Planetary Mission

Space Exploration Technologies Corp. is the most successful rocket company in human history. SpaceX pioneered reusable rocket technology with Falcon 9, making orbital launches routine and affordable. Starship — the largest and most powerful rocket ever built — is designed to carry humans to Mars and beyond. With Starlink, SpaceX is simultaneously building the world's largest satellite internet constellation.

  • First private company to send humans to the ISS (2020)
  • Falcon 9 booster recovered and reflown 20+ times
  • Starship successfully reached orbit and executed full flight profile
  • 7,000+ Starlink satellites in orbit, covering 100+ countries
  • 400+ total orbital launches and counting

Tesla

Founded 2003 · Austin, Texas

Starlink

Launched 2019 · Global Coverage

Connecting the World

Starlink is SpaceX's low-Earth orbit satellite internet constellation, delivering high-speed broadband to underserved and remote areas worldwide. With over 7,000 satellites in orbit and growing, Starlink has connected rural families, disaster relief zones, maritime vessels, airlines, and military operations. It is the fastest-growing internet service provider in history and is bringing connectivity to billions who had none.

Neuralink

Founded 2016 · Fremont, California

Restoring Human Potential

Neuralink is developing ultra-high bandwidth brain-computer interfaces designed to restore full-body functionality to people with paralysis and neurological conditions. The N1 implant — a coin-sized chip placed by a surgical robot — allows paralyzed patients to control computers, play games, and communicate using only their thoughts. Long-term, Neuralink envisions a merger of human cognition with AI, unlocking capabilities beyond biological limits.

  • FDA Breakthrough Device designation received
  • Multiple patients controlling computers and gaming with BCI
  • Paralyzed patient regained digital communication and chess play
  • R1 surgical robot achieves sub-millimeter precision implants
  • PRIME study expanded with additional trial participants

The Boring Company

Founded 2016 · Bastrop, Texas

Solving Urban Traffic

The Boring Company was founded on the belief that traffic is a solvable problem — by going underground. Using Elon's philosophy of first principles, TBC has reinvented tunnel boring to be dramatically faster and cheaper than traditional methods. The Las Vegas Loop system shuttles passengers beneath the convention center at speeds impossible above ground. Projects in multiple cities signal a new era of urban mobility.

  • Tunneling cost reduced 10x vs industry average

DOGE

Established 2025 · Washington, D.C.

Government Efficiency

The Department of Government Efficiency is Elon Musk's bold effort to bring Silicon Valley-speed accountability to the federal government. DOGE applied the same first-principles thinking that built SpaceX and Tesla to identify waste, redundancy, and inefficiency across federal agencies. The project represents the most significant attempt at government modernization in modern American history — cutting bureaucracy and returning taxpayer money.

  • $160B+ in government savings identified and documented
  • Thousands of redundant federal contracts cancelled
  • Streamlined multiple federal agency operations
  • Real-time spending transparency dashboard launched
  • First-principles audit of federal workforce efficiency
