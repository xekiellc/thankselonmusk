From Pretoria to Mars

THE ELON MUSK TIMELINE

Every founding, launch, milestone, and moonshot — from a kid teaching himself to code to the most consequential builder in human history.

1971
Origins
Born in Pretoria, South Africa
Elon Reeve Musk born June 28, 1971. Shows early aptitude for reading and computers.
1983
Early Life
Sells First Software at Age 12
Self-taught on a Commodore computer, Elon creates and sells a video game called Blastar for $500.
1992
Early Life
Moves to Canada, Then University of Pennsylvania
Earns dual bachelor's degrees in economics and physics. Begins PhD at Stanford — leaves after two days to start companies.
1995
Zip2
Co-founds Zip2
First company — a web software firm providing online city guides for newspapers. Sold to Compaq for $307M in 1999. Elon's share: $22M.
1999
X.com / PayPal
Founds X.com — Becomes PayPal
Online financial services company merges with Confinity to become PayPal. eBay acquires it for $1.5B in 2002. Elon nets $180M.
2002
SpaceX
Founds Space Exploration Technologies
Invests $100M of personal fortune. Mission: make humanity multi-planetary. Industry experts give it near-zero odds of success.
2004
Tesla
Joins Tesla Motors as Chairman
Leads Series A funding round of $7.5M. Takes over as CEO in 2008. Sets mission: accelerate sustainable energy transition.
2008
SpaceX
Falcon 1 Reaches Orbit — First Private Rocket
After three failures nearly bankrupting SpaceX, Falcon 1 becomes the first privately developed liquid-fueled rocket to reach orbit.
2010
SpaceX
Dragon Capsule Reaches Orbit and Returns
First private spacecraft to be recovered from orbit. Sets the stage for ISS cargo and crew missions.
2012
Tesla
Model S Launches — Changes Everything
Named Motor Trend Car of the Year. Earns highest Consumer Reports score ever recorded. Proves EVs can be premium, desirable vehicles.
2015
SpaceX
Falcon 9 Booster Lands Itself for First Time
Orbital rocket booster returns and lands vertically — a feat declared impossible by aerospace industry. Reusability era begins.
2016
Neuralink
Co-founds Neuralink
Brain-computer interface company aimed at restoring mobility for paralyzed patients and eventually merging human cognition with AI.
Boring Co
Founds The Boring Company
After tweeting frustration about LA traffic, Elon starts a tunneling company applying first principles to reduce costs 10x.
2018
SpaceX
Falcon Heavy Launches — Most Powerful Rocket Since Saturn V
Carries Elon's personal Tesla Roadster to Mars orbit with a mannequin named Starman. All three boosters recovered.
2019
Starlink
First Starlink Satellites Launched
60 satellites deployed in initial batch. Begins building what becomes the world's largest satellite constellation.
2020
SpaceX
First Private Crewed Mission to the ISS
Crew Dragon launches NASA astronauts Bob Behnken and Doug Hurley. SpaceX becomes first private company to send humans to orbit.
2021
Tesla
Tesla Reaches $1 Trillion Market Cap
Joins the exclusive club of trillion-dollar companies. Elon becomes world's richest person for the first time.
2022
X
Acquires Twitter for $44B — Renames it X
Restores free speech, reinstates banned accounts, cuts costs by 80%, introduces creator monetization and Community Notes.
2023
xAI
Founds xAI — Launches Grok
Creates truth-seeking AI company. Grok integrates into X for hundreds of millions of users. Raises $6B+ in funding.
2024
Neuralink
First Human Neuralink Patient Implanted
Noland Arbaugh, paralyzed from shoulders down, controls a computer cursor with his thoughts. Games, browses, communicates independently.
SpaceX
Starship Completes Full Orbital Flight
After iterative testing, Starship executes a complete flight profile including booster catch by the Mechazilla arm — a world first.
2025
DOGE
Leads Department of Government Efficiency
Applies first-principles thinking to federal government. Identifies $160B+ in savings. Most significant government audit in modern history.
xAI
Grok 3 Released with Advanced Reasoning
xAI's Colossus supercomputer — 100,000+ H100 GPUs — powers Grok 3, competing with the world's frontier AI models.
2026 →
SpaceX
Mars Mission on the Horizon
Starship on track for first uncrewed Mars mission. Humanity's multi-planetary future moves from vision to imminent reality.
